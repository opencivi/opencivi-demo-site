About Rosa's Catering Co.

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded in 2005 with a simple belief: that the quality of food at a corporate event should match the importance of the occasion. What began as a small operation serving downtown Chicago law firms has grown into the Chicago metro area's premier B2B catering partner.

Today our team of professional chefs and event coordinators manages hundreds of corporate accounts — from daily office lunch programs at startups to multi-day conference catering for Fortune 500 companies. Every menu is crafted fresh, every dietary need is accommodated, and every event is executed with precision.

We work exclusively with businesses. No wedding receptions, no private parties — just a relentless focus on making your workplace and corporate events exceptional.

— Rosa & the Catering Co. Team
