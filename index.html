About Us

Rosa's Catering Co. was born from a simple belief: that great food transforms a meeting into a memory. Founded in 2008 by Rosa Marchetti, we started by catering a handful of local office lunches and quickly grew into the region's most trusted corporate caterer.

Today, our team of 30 culinary professionals serves over 200 corporate clients — from startups hosting their first all-hands to Fortune 500 companies running multi-day conferences. Every menu is crafted from scratch using seasonal, locally sourced ingredients.

We handle everything: consultation, menu planning, staffing, setup, and cleanup — so your team can focus entirely on what matters.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder & Executive Chef
