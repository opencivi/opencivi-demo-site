About Us

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded on a simple belief: the food at your next corporate event should be just as impressive as the agenda. After two decades of cooking for families, Rosa Marchetti pivoted to corporate catering in 2015 — bringing the same homemade quality and warm hospitality to boardrooms, conference halls, and office kitchens across Chicago.

Today, our team of professional chefs and event coordinators serves over 200 companies — from fast-growing startups to Fortune 500 firms. We handle everything from menu planning and dietary accommodations to on-site setup, service, and teardown, so you can focus entirely on your team.

Every dish is made fresh the morning of your event. No pre-packaged shortcuts. No reheated compromises. Just food your team will talk about long after the meeting ends.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder
