About Rosa's Catering Co.

Founded in 2008 by Rosa Marchetti after 15 years in Chicago's fine dining scene, Rosa's Catering Co. was built on a simple belief: corporate dining should be as exceptional as the business it supports. We specialize exclusively in B2B clients — from startup all-hands lunches to Fortune 500 gala dinners.

Our team of certified executive chefs crafts seasonal, locally sourced menus tailored to your brand and guests. Every event is assigned a dedicated coordinator who manages logistics end-to-end, so your team can focus on what matters most.

We serve the greater Chicago metro area and have catered over 4,000 corporate events. Dietary accommodations — including vegan, gluten-free, and kosher options — are always available at no extra charge.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder & Executive Chef
