About Rosa's Catering Co.

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded by Rosa Marchetti in 2005 with a clear mission: to bring restaurant-quality food and hospitality to the corporate world. After years running a beloved neighborhood kitchen, Rosa saw an unmet need — businesses deserved better than boxed lunches and grocery-store trays.

Today, our team of 25 culinary professionals serves over 200 corporate clients across the metro area, from nimble tech startups to Fortune 500 headquarters. Whether it's a weekly office lunch program or a 500-person annual gala, we deliver flawless food and service every single time.

Fully licensed, insured, and ServSafe-certified. We partner directly with office managers, HR teams, and event coordinators to make every engagement completely effortless on your end.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder & Executive Chef
