About Us

Rosa Marchetti founded Rosa's Catering Co. with a simple belief: that great food transforms a meeting into a moment. Starting with a handful of local clients in 2008, we've grown into Chicago's most trusted corporate catering partner — serving over 500 companies, from startups to Fortune 500 firms.

Every menu is customized to your event. Our in-house culinary team handles everything from sourcing seasonal ingredients to coordinating seamless on-site delivery and service, so your team can focus on what matters.

We specialize exclusively in B2B catering — no restaurants, no retail. Your account comes with a dedicated event coordinator and guaranteed 24-hour quote turnaround.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder & CEO
