About Rosa's Catering Co.

Rosa Marchetti built her catering company on a simple conviction: corporate food doesn't have to be forgettable. After two decades running a beloved neighborhood restaurant, she turned her attention to the office — where meals are eaten over laptops and catering trays arrive cold and uninspired.

Today, Rosa's Catering Co. partners with over 200 companies across the metro area, from early-stage startups to Fortune 500 teams. Every menu is customized, every dish is made fresh that morning, and every delivery includes full setup and cleanup — so your team can focus on the meeting, not the logistics.

We handle office lunches, all-hands events, client receptions, and annual galas. If it feeds a team, we've got it covered.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder
