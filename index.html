About Rosa's Catering Co.

Founded in 2005 by Rosa Marchetti, Rosa's Catering Co. began with a simple mission: bring restaurant-quality food to the boardroom. What started as a small operation serving a handful of Chicago-area offices has grown into a trusted partner for over 300 companies — from scrappy startups to Fortune 500 firms.

Every menu is crafted fresh using locally sourced ingredients. Our culinary team works directly with your event coordinator to design meals that fit your headcount, budget, dietary needs, and brand. No cookie-cutter catering — every order is tailored to you.

We handle everything: delivery, setup, service, and cleanup. Your team shows up to a great meal. We take care of the rest.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder & Head Chef
