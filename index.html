About Us

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded on a simple belief: that the food served at your company events should be just as impressive as the work happening inside. Rosa Marchetti brought her decades of culinary experience — and her family's recipes — into the corporate world, building a catering operation that Fortune 500s and startups alike trust for their most important moments.

Today our team of 30 culinary professionals serves over 200 corporate clients across the metro area. From quick office lunches to black-tie gala dinners, every event receives the same meticulous attention to detail, seasonally sourced ingredients, and Rosa's personal touch.

We handle setup, service, and breakdown so your team can focus on what matters. Fully licensed, insured, and ServSafe certified. Dedicated account managers for clients booking 4+ events per year.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder & Executive Chef
