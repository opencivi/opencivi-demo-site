About Us

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded in 2005 with a simple belief: companies deserve the same quality of food as the finest restaurants. What began as small office lunch deliveries for a handful of Chicago startups has grown into one of the city's most trusted corporate catering partners.

Today, our team of professional chefs and event coordinators serves over 200 corporate clients — from boutique agencies to Fortune 500 companies. We handle everything from weekly office lunches to multi-day conference catering and black-tie gala dinners.

Every menu is crafted with seasonal, locally sourced ingredients. We manage all dietary requirements, logistics, and on-site execution so your team can focus on what matters most: your business.

— Rosa & The Catering Team
