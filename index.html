About Us

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded on a simple belief: that great food elevates every business occasion. From a single corporate account in 2005, we've grown into Chicago's most trusted B2B catering partner — serving Fortune 500 boardrooms, startup all-hands events, and everything in between.

Our culinary team crafts every menu from scratch using locally sourced ingredients. We handle logistics end-to-end — from initial consultation and custom menu design to on-site setup, professional service, and full cleanup — so your team can focus on what matters.

We specialize in seamless, repeatable service that reflects well on your company. Every dietary restriction is accommodated. Every deadline is met. Every event leaves a lasting impression.

— Rosa & the Team
