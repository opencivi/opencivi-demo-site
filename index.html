About Us

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: bring restaurant-quality food and white-glove service to the corporate world. What started as a small operation serving a handful of Chicago law firms has grown into one of the region's most trusted B2B catering partners.

Today, our team of professional chefs, event coordinators, and service staff cater to over 200 companies — from growing startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. We understand that a great meal can set the tone for a great meeting, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Every menu is crafted fresh on the day of your event. Every team member is trained to deliver seamless, discreet service that lets you focus on what matters — your business.

— Rosa & The Team
