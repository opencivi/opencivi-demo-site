About Rosa's Catering Co.

Founded in 2009, Rosa's Catering Co. was built on a single conviction: that corporate food should never feel like an afterthought. Rosa Delgado launched the company after years running high-end restaurant kitchens, bringing the same culinary discipline to boardrooms and conference halls across Chicago.

Today, our team of professional chefs and event coordinators serves over 200 corporate clients — from intimate 10-person team lunches to 500-guest gala dinners. Every menu is tailored, every delivery is on time, and every dietary need is accommodated without compromise.

We handle the food so you can focus on the meeting. Seamless, reliable, and delicious — that's our guarantee to every client, every time.

— Rosa Delgado, Founder & Executive Chef
