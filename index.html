About Rosa Catering

Founded in 2008 by Rosa Marchetti, Rosa Catering was built on a single belief: every employee deserves a restaurant-quality meal, even at the office. Starting with a single corporate client in Minato-ku, we have grown to serve over 200 companies across the Greater Tokyo Area.

Our team of professional chefs prepares every dish fresh each day, using seasonal ingredients sourced directly from trusted local suppliers. From intimate boardroom lunches to large-scale annual parties, we deliver the same unwavering commitment to quality and service.

We specialize exclusively in B2B corporate catering. Every detail — menu, timing, presentation — is tailored to your company's needs, schedule, and budget.

— Rosa Catering Team
