About Us

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded with a single conviction: that corporate dining should never be an afterthought. We partner with businesses of every size — from agile startups to Fortune 500 firms — to deliver culinary experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Our team of professional chefs and event coordinators handle every detail, from custom menu design to on-site setup and service. We source seasonal, high-quality ingredients and tailor every event to your brand, culture, and dietary requirements.

Whether you're feeding a team of 15 for a weekly lunch or hosting 500 guests at an annual conference, Rosa's brings the same commitment to quality, punctuality, and presentation to every engagement.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder & Executive Chef
