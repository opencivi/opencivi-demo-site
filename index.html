About Us

Rosa's Catering Co. was founded in 2008 by Rosa Marchetti, who brought her family's Italian culinary tradition to the world of corporate dining. What began as small office lunch deliveries to a handful of downtown Chicago firms has grown into a full-service catering operation trusted by over 200 companies across the metro area.

Our culinary team sources premium seasonal ingredients and crafts every menu with the same care Rosa's family has practiced for generations. We handle everything — consultation, custom menus, setup, staffing, and cleanup — so your team can focus on what matters.

From weekly office lunches to high-stakes board dinners, we understand that corporate dining is a reflection of your company's values. We take that responsibility seriously.

— Rosa Marchetti, Founder
